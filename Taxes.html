Tax Preparation Companies Near You

Websites


Turbo Tax


TurboTax uses a Interview-style Q&A experience to help the user file their taxes in a easy to understand system. They offer a wide rande of functionality and tools that makes entering information easy. Furthermore, help is easy to access, and explanations are clear and authorative.

  • Pricing: free-$90 for federal; 40$ per state; free- $170 for TurboTax Live; additional $45-60 for Max
  • Additional info: user-friendly; live, on-screen support options offers human help at extra cost; more expensive compared to other competitors.

H&R Block


H&R Block is a comprehensive, easy-to-use tax service that helps e-filers claim relevant income, deductions, and credits. It's an excellent choice for simple and comples returns, both in terms of its user experience and help options.

  • Pricing: free-$85 for federal; $37 per state; $40-145 for Online Assist
  • Additional info: straightforward and easy to use; free version is an option for more people compared to other competitors; access to tax pros on demand at an added cost.

Jackson Hewitt Online


In addition to a dead-simple interface, a comprehensive library of tax articles, and built-in calculators, Jackson Hewitt Online offers the best refunding insurance. The service features free IRS audit assistance, a Worry-Free Guarentee plan, along with a Worry-Free Guarantee Platinum tier plan which inclues Audit Security support from a dedicated support specialist to manage and represent your case.

  • Pricing: $25 for federal; $49 per state; $135 for W2 forms; $300 for 1099 forms; etc..
  • Additional info: unlimited email and live chat support; easy-to-navigate interface; can be more expensive than other competitors; fewer features than other services.

Tax Slayer


TaxSlayer is an affordable tax preparation service and provides all the tax forms you'll need. That noted, the free version comes with some degree of payment to access personalized tax advice. TaxSlayer Premium provides comprehensive assistance from a tax professional, including live phone and chat support, as well as audit protection.

  • Pricing: free-$47 for federal; $39 per state
  • Additional info: TaxSlayer offers a no-interest advance on your tax refund (after the IRS accepted your filing); could save a lot of money if you're a confident tax filer.

Tax Act


TaxAct is less well-known among other tax software providers, but it offers quality online software that general cost far less. It's strengths lie on its simplicity, navigation options, and the ability to browse through the knowledge database as you input your itemized deductions.

  • Pricing: free-$65 for federal; $5-45 per state filing; extra cost for Tax Specialist support
  • Additional info: Prices are set based on level of support rather than level of functionality; cheaper than other competitors; fewer real-time support options - especially face-to-face ones.

Credit Karma


Credit Karma Tax is a completely free along with a user-friendly interface that provides a helpful guidance about any tax related terminology. It supports most IRS forms and schedules for federal and state returns, even the Schedule C.

  • Pricing: $0 for feredal and state, 100% free
  • Additional info: software limitations like: unable to file taxes without filing your federal taxes first, doesn't support multiple state filing, part-year state filing or non-resident.
